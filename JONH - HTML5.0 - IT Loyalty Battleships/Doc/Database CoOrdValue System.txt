IT Loyalty BattleShips Cell Values:

	Value:		Graphical Meaning:
	O		Grid cell is blank (No local or opposition ship or hit component)
	X		Grid cell has been hit by the opposition player but contains no local or opposition ship piece
	S		Grid cell contains a local player ship piece
	H		Grid cell contains a local player ship piece and it has been hit by the oposition player
	E		Grid cell has been hit by the local player and it does contain the opposition player ship piece
	M		Grid cell has been hit by the local player and it does contain the opposition player ship piece and local player ship piece
